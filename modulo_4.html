Caio's Mission 2:

3. NEW CONCEPT: The Past Continuous

No setor de Óleo e Gás, investigar incidentes é rotina. O Past Continuous descreve uma ação que estava acontecendo (em progresso) quando outra ação cruzou o caminho. Formamos com: Was/Were + Verbo com ING.

• "The engineers were inspecting the drill when the alarm rang."
(Os engenheiros estavam inspecionando a broca quando o alarme tocou.)

• "I was analyzing the supply chain report while the CEO was talking."
(Eu estava analisando o relatório de logística enquanto o CEO falava.)

Caio, explain to Matheus: Notice that the "background action" is long (Past Continuous), and the "interruption" is short (Simple Past).

4. Petrobras Roleplays (Flipped Context)

Caio, you will guide these dialogues. Correct Matheus if he makes a mistake in the timeline. Speak clearly and with authority.

Simulation 1: Offshore Rig (The Incident)
Caio (Manager) "Matheus, I read your incident report. What exactly happened yesterday?"
Matheus (Engineer) "We were drilling the new well when the pressure dropped suddenly."
Caio (Manager) "Were the technicians monitoring the gauges?"
Matheus (Engineer) "Yes, they were looking at the system. The software crashed."
Caio (Manager) "This is unacceptable. Are we going to replace the software?"
Matheus (Engineer) "Yes, the IT department is preparing the new patch right now."
Caio (Manager) "Good. I will call the safety director to update him."
Simulation 2: Supply Chain & Logistics
Caio (Coordinator) "Matheus, where is the shipment from the onshore facility?"
Matheus (Logistics) "It is delayed. The trucks were traveling on the highway when the storm started."
Caio (Coordinator) "But the refinery needs those parts today! What are you going to do?"
Matheus (Logistics) "I will hire a private helicopter to transport the critical valves."
Caio (Coordinator) "Is the CFO going to approve this cost?"
Matheus (Logistics) "Yes, I spoke with him yesterday. He approved the emergency budget."
Caio (Coordinator) "Perfect. Keep me updated."
Simulation 3: Administrative Boardroom (Future Plans)
Matheus (Director) "Caio, can you present the forecast for the next quarter?"
Caio (Analyst) "Certainly. We are going to increase production by 8% in the pre-salt layer."
Matheus (Director) "How did you reach that number?"
Caio (Analyst) "I was analyzing the extraction data last week. The new platforms are very efficient."
Matheus (Director) "Did we hire the new crew for Platform P-75?"
Caio (Analyst) "HR is interviewing the candidates right now. We will finalize the team by Friday."
Matheus (Director) "Excellent job, Caio."
Simulation 4: Environmental & Safety Audit
Caio (Auditor) "Matheus, I am conducting the quarterly safety audit. Answer honestly."
Matheus (Supervisor) "Of course. Safety is our priority."
Caio (Auditor) "What were the workers doing when the leak happened on Monday?"
Matheus (Supervisor) "They were replacing a pipe. We stopped the operation immediately."
Caio (Auditor) "Did you report this to the environmental agency?"
Matheus (Supervisor) "Yes, I sent the forms yesterday afternoon."
Caio (Auditor) "Okay. I will review the documents and send you the final assessment."
Simulation 5: The Daily Stand-up (Mixed)
Matheus "Good morning, team. Caio, what is your status?"
Caio "Yesterday, I finished the contract with the new supplier."
Matheus "Are they going to deliver the materials on time?"
Caio "Yes. Right now, they are loading the ships at the port."
Matheus "What were you discussing with the engineers earlier?"
Caio "We were planning the maintenance schedule. I will send the Excel file later."
Matheus "Great alignment. Let's get to work."
