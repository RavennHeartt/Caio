1. Infinitivo vs. Imperativo: O Segredo do "TO"

Por que os professores sempre falam de "To Be", "To Work"? No inglês, o TO antes do verbo funciona como o nosso -AR, -ER, -IR no português (Infinitivo). É o verbo em seu estado puro, sem ação.

Se você tirar o "To", o verbo se torna um Imperativo (uma ordem direta) ou aguarda um sujeito para o Simple Present.

• To sell: Vender

• Sell this car now! Venda este carro agora! (Imperativo)

• To hire: Contratar

• Hire him! Contrate ele!

The Simple Present Review

Para descrever sua rotina no trabalho, basta juntar o Sujeito + Verbo (sem o "To"). Lembre-se: He/She/It ganha o -S.

• I negotiate contracts. / He negotiates contracts.

2. Simple Past & O Terror do "Was / Were"

No corporativo, você é medido pelo que entregou. Usamos o Simple Past para ações mortas e concluídas.

The Verb To Be in the Past (Ser / Estar)

O verbo To Be no passado não usa "-ed". Ele se divide em duas formas absolutas. Decore isso para suas entrevistas:

WAS (Era / Estava / Foi): Singular total.

• I / He / She / It WAS.

Ex: I was at the meeting. / The interview was great.

WERE (Eram / Estavam / Fomos): Plural e o "Você".

• You / We / They WERE.

Ex: We were ready. / You were hired.

Action Verbs (Regulars & Rebels)

Regulars (-ED): Finish → Finished | Approve → Approved.

Irregulars (Mudam a forma): Have → Had | Go → Went | Meet → Met.

3. Reading Challenge: The Corporate Report

Leia o texto abaixo. Identifique quais ações são rotina (Present) e quais já aconteceram (Past). Preste atenção aos cognatos.

The global department manages a complex operation. Every morning, the system processes thousands of data points automatically. However, yesterday, the main server failed. The executive director was furious. Caio contacted the engineering team immediately. They were in a meeting, but they solved the critical issue in ten minutes. Today, the platform works perfectly again and the client needs the final report. We have a great team!

4. Real World Roleplays (Exhaustive Practice)

Intercale os papéis com o professor. Leia com entonação profissional. O objetivo aqui é volume e resistência.

Simulation 1: The HR Hallway (Post-Interview)
Matheus "Hey Caio! Over here! Did you finish your interview?"
Caio "Matheus! Yes, I just finished. It was intense."
Matheus "How was the HR manager? Was she difficult?"
Caio "No, she was very professional. She asked about my past experience."
Matheus "And what did you say?"
Caio "I explained that I managed a team of five people last year."
Matheus "That is perfect! They need leadership. Did they offer the job?"
Caio "Yes! They offered the coordinator position. I accepted immediately."
Matheus "Congratulations! We are officially colleagues now."
Caio "Thank you! I am very excited. We need to celebrate later."
Simulation 2: Lunchtime at the Cafeteria
Caio "Is this seat taken?"
Matheus "No, sit down! How is your first week going?"
Caio "It is busy. I organize my tasks in the morning and review reports in the afternoon."
Matheus "Did you meet the IT director yesterday?"
Caio "Yes, I met him. We had a long conversation about the software."
Matheus "He is very strict. What did he say?"
Caio "He said the system was slow. So, I analyzed the code and found the problem."
Matheus "Did you fix it already?"
Caio "Yes, I deployed the solution this morning."
Matheus "Wow. You are fast. This food is terrible, by the way."
Caio "I agree. Tomorrow we buy food outside!"
Simulation 3: Sales Growth Meeting
Matheus "Welcome everyone. Caio, can you present the numbers for Q3?"
Caio "Of course. Last quarter, we changed our approach. The results were excellent."
Matheus "Show us the data. Did we hit the target?"
Caio "Yes. We surpassed the target by 15%. The new strategy worked."
Matheus "Why did sales increase so much in September?"
Caio "We launched the new campaign, and the marketing team was aggressive on social media."
Matheus "Did the clients respond well to the pricing?"
Caio "Yes, they loved the discount. We sold 500 units in one week."
Matheus "Fantastic job. I want this replicated for Q4."
Caio "Understood. I will prepare the new projections by tomorrow."
Simulation 4: Boss's Office (Marketing Shift)
Caio "Matheus, do you have a minute?"
Matheus "Come in, Caio. What is on your mind?"
Caio "I reviewed the marketing flow yesterday. I noticed a big bottleneck."
Matheus "Really? Where is the problem?"
Caio "The designers wait too long for the copywriters. The process was very slow last month."
Matheus "What do you suggest?"
Caio "To implement a parallel system. They work at the same time using a cloud document."
Matheus "Did you test this idea?"
Caio "Yes, I tested it with a small team last Friday. Efficiency increased by 30%."
Matheus "I like it. You have my approval. Change the protocol today."
Caio "Thank you. I will send the email right now."
Simulation 5: High-End Car Negotiation
Caio "Good afternoon, sir. Welcome to the dealership. How can I help you?"
Matheus "Good afternoon. I am looking for a sports car. I saw the V8 model online."
Caio "Excellent choice. That car is a beast. Did you drive a V8 before?"
Matheus "Yes, I had a Mustang two years ago, but I sold it."
Caio "I understand. Our new model has 500 horsepower. It goes from 0 to 100 in 3.5 seconds."
Matheus "That is impressive. How was the safety rating in the last test?"
Caio "It was five stars. The engineers improved the braking system completely."
Matheus "What about the price? The website price was a bit high."
Caio "To be honest, the market changed. But if we close the deal today, I can offer a 5% discount and free insurance for a year."
Matheus "Can we do a test drive first?"
Caio "Of course. Let me grab the keys. Follow me."
